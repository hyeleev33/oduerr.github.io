About Me

Servus, I am Oliver Dürr a theoretical / computational physicist by training. I have been using computers and mathematics for solving all kinds of interesting problems for almost 20 years now. I am especially fascinated about data analysis and statistical learning (a.k.a. machine learning). In my research I am currently focusing on deep learning.

I work as an associate professor / senior lecturer (Dozent) for statistical data analysis at the Zurich University of Applied Sciences (ZHAW) in Winterthur, Switzerland. My homebase is the Institute of Data Analysis and Process Design (IDP). You can find me in my office here.
