About Me

Hello, I am Oliver Dürr a theoretical / computational physicist by training. I am applying math using computers to all kind of interesting problems since nearly 20 years now. I am especially fascinated in data analysis and statistical learning (a.k.a. machine learning) and what is now called data science. In my research I am currently focusing on deep learning.

I work as an associate professor / senior lecturer (dozent) for statistical data analysis at the Zurich University of Applied Sciences (ZHAW) in Winterthur. My homebase is the institute of data analysis and process design (IDP). You can find me in my office here.
